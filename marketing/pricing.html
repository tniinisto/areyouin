R'YouIN license

There is a free first month trial period and after that a license fee in order to continue the usage. After purchase the license is updated immediately and obtained days are added on top of the existing license. Usage can continue without delay. If the license has expired, it can be purchased after application login and usage can continue normally after the payment. With this license policy the application is kept advertisement free and the usage therefore more convenient.

License can be purchased with three different currencies: EUR, USD or GBP. Euros are used as the base currency. US Dollar and British Pound license values are calculated from the days exchange rate. Finish value added tax (VAT) is added to the prices on the payment and tax is separated also on the receipt. Paypal is used as the payment service.
